History

The Region's economic growth in the last several years has resulted in increased opportunities for the construction works and installation of high-end electrical systems and Home Automation. PROJECTA is positioned to capitalize on the growing need of businesses in this Area. Established in 2006, PROJECTA started in healthcare and it has steadily progressed into construction field. PROJECTA has grown into a multi-divisional organization that practice business across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The adherence to highly quality standards of services and the strong financial position enabled PROJECTA to maintain its distinctive reputation among the leading companies in its field of businesses. PROJECTA employs executives, professional managers, and high calibre skilled multinational staff whose disciplines cover every aspect of the Constructions, Operations, Maintenance and Space Automation sectors.
